My love,

I don’t know if you’ll ever read everything that was meant for you… I’m not waiting for an answer. I haven’t been for a long time. I’m not trying to spark pity, and even less trying to awaken guilt in you. I just want you to know.

I hope you won’t forget me. I hope you’ll remember, at least sometimes. Maybe when you hear that song by The Beatles - All My Loving.

It’s not kilometers that separate us now, it’s time.

To be honest, I barely believe you’ll ever read any of this. Hence the name: Ethereal Reverie. It feels like I’m speaking to myself… or maybe sending out light across the cosmos, hoping that somewhere beyond the edges of time, you’ll catch this signal.
Like particles existing in two places at once, my words drift between realities - between me… and you.

— V.
