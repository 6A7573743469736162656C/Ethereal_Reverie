April 12, 2025

It’s raining outside. That scent - damp air, wet pavements suddenly brought me back to 2013, when I was just five years old.
I remember… it was autumn, in a town near Moscow called Podolsk. My dad picked me up from kindergarten, we drove home in our blue Honda. On the radio, "Coming Home" by Skylar Grey & Diddy was playing. I was filled with excitement, eager to get home, grab my PSP... It had a photo of a helicopter as the wallpaper. It was loaded with games and music.

Back then, I had no idea I’d one day be living in Spain, speaking Spanish, walking under different skies.
And where were you in that moment, my love? Do you ever think about that part of your childhood?

Lately, I’ve been thinking a lot about my past... not in a sad way, but with deep nostalgia.
These memories are some of my most intimate, sacred ones. No one in the world knows them like I do… not even my family would remember the details. But I want to share them with you. I don’t know why… I just feel this kind of urge.

— V.
