Poetry

These are my poems.

I want to apologize for deleting my old ones. Only one from that time remains - Whispers of Serenity.

Lately, I’ve been writing just for myself, because I doubt you’ll ever read them. That’s why I write in the language where I can express my feelings most deeply, my native language - Russian.

But if you ever do read my poems, I want you to know… in translation, the feelings don’t come through. Everything is lost - the charm, the emotions… all of it.

13.06.2023

Whispers of Serenity: A Solitary Sojourn in the Forest

In the forest, on a bench I rest,
No souls around, alone, I'm blessed.
One with nature, in solitude's embrace,
A rarity now, this tranquil space.

Leaves gently rustle, whispers in the air,
Nature's symphony, a melody rare.
In the distance, branches break with might,
The weight of beastly paws, a primal sight.

Oh, such idylls are seldom found,
In a bustling world where chaos resounds.
But here, in this haven, peace abounds,
A moment of stillness, where solace is found.

I cherish this solitude, this precious hour,
With nature's wonders, my spirit does devour.
In this forest sanctuary, I find release,
A fleeting respite, a moment of peace.
