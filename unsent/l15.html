November 23, 2025

Today there was such a beautiful sunset. How many sunsets have we spent together? Every sunset i watch alone resonates with you and each time my heart gets heavier because it is overflowing with feelings for you.
I often say that I want to hug you, hold your hand, and simply feel your gaze on me, but i hardly remember ever saying that i want to kiss you. I believe… its too intimate. But yes. Obviously, i do want to. Not with excessive passion. Gently and tenderly, as if afraid to melt the snow with the warmth of my lips.
After all, a kiss is something sacred… that must be treasured and cherished. Honestly I don’t really know how to speak about a kiss because it is a heartbeat-long moment. But its a moment, for which, everything exists.

— V.
