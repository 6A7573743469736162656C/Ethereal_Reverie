101 Reasons Why I Love You

1. Because you were my muse from the very beginning even in silence.

2. Because you could feel me without needing words.

3. For the invisible thread that held us together, even from afar.

4. For those “just a glance” moments that said more than anything.

5. For the way you spoke to me through music and lyrics.

6. For that moment when you answered "Do it" and the world paused.

7. Because that lyric game we played made me believe in magic.

8. Because you made me a poet.

9. Because your notes on Instagram were warmer than sunlight.

10. For that one spring when I knew I was truly loved.

11. For your smile that reached through the screen and touched my soul.

12. Because your mind was a galaxy I never finished exploring.

13. Because even when we weren’t talking, you were still with me.

14. Because something in me always knew you were thinking of me too.

15. Because you made me feel wanted, without ever saying it out loud.

16. For every time you pretended not to care, but did deeply.

17. For your fearless softness.

18. For your eyes, the ones I still see when I close mine.

19. Because you weren’t just a girl, you were a turning point in my life.

20. Because I couldn’t forget you, no matter how hard I tried.

21. Because after you, no one else felt real.

22. Because you became the standard of beauty and soul.

23. Because your silence made me realize how much I loved your words.

24. For that invisible electricity in our every near-touch.

25. Because you saw something in me no one else did.

26. Because you made me dream again.

27. Because without you, even dreams faded.

28. For the night you were in my headphones and my heart.

29. For the way you made even the simplest moments feel like they mattered.

30. Because I’d give anything to hear you say my name again.

31. Because I still remember the scent of your perfume.

32. Because your laughter is the loveliest sound I’ve ever known.

33. For the phrases you said that still echo in my mind.

34. Because you are the first person I ever truly prayed for.

35. Because with you, even silence was a conversation.

36. Because you taught me how to wait.

37. Because you showed me love isn’t just joy, it’s depth.

38. For the way you touched me, not with hands, but words.

39. Because every thought of you made me want to be a better man.

40. Because you are the brightest mark in my life.

41. For the way your depth was hidden behind an effortless smile.

42. For your depth, the kind I never reached the bottom of.

43. For how you could quiet my soul and awaken it at the same time.

44. Because in every fleeting moment, you made me feel understood.

45. For the way you looked at me, as if I was someone worth loving.

46. Because you made me believe I could be loved.

47. Because you are my springtime.

48. Because you are a memory I protect like something sacred.

49. Because you live in every poem I write, even when I don’t say your name.

50. Because you are the reason I started writing again.

51. Because without you, even music sounds different.

52. Because you made me feel alive.

53. For that almost-kiss more powerful than any kiss we’ve ever had.

54. Because you aren’t like anyone else.

55. Because even your shadow was light to me.

56. Because you were the spark that ignited something I didn’t know I needed.

57. For the way one message from you could save my entire day.

58. For the way your presence could change the atmosphere, without saying a word.

59. For the way you loved fiercely, without reservation.

60. Because I want to protect you from everything.

61. Because you were both strong and intelligent, and you wore it with grace.

62. Because you knew exactly where my heart was.

63. Because you took my heart with you.

64. For the mornings when we thought of each other without telling each other.

65. Because you were in the most important dreams.

66. Because even when I wasn’t texting, I was thinking about you.

67. Because I never truly fell out of love with you.

68. Because I want to be with you in another lifetime too.

69. Because every single sunrise without you felt empty.

70. Because you aren’t just a chapter, you are the meaning of the book.

71. For your eyelashes.

72. For your lips.

73. For the way you said "okay".

74. For the way you flirted.

75. Because I knew you were mine, even if the world didn’t know.

76. Because you never betrayed me.

77. Because you were always honest.

78. Because you are gentle.

79. Because you are real.

80. Because you were the reason I started believing in fate.

81. Because I love you more every day.

82. Because love didn’t die, it just learned how to stay quiet.

83. Because I’d go through it all again, just to be close to you once more.

84. Because I still hear you in my favorite songs.

85. Because no city feels right without you in it.

86. Because I keep looking for your rhythm in other people, but they don’t have it.

87. Because you are ART.

88. Because you are God’s gift to my soul.

89. Because you are light in my darkness.

90. Because you are the most beautiful part of my life.

91. Because you’re heartbreakingly beautiful.

92. Because your soul is even more beautiful.

95. Because you gave me more than anyone ever did.

96. Because you are my missing half.

97. Because you were my spring, my autumn, and everything in between.

98. Because I still pray for you, even though I don’t know where you are.

99. Because I want you to be happy, even if it’s not with me.

100. Because no matter how much time passes, a part of me will always belong to you.

101. Because I will always love you. Truly. Quietly. Eternally.

With all my heart,
— V.
