April 13, 2025

I like soft kisses.

There’s something magical in them. Not too much passion... just something tender, something pure...
That first touch of lips… APOCALYPSE
So gentle, so soft, yet shaking the very fabric of your soul.
It does feel like tulips blooming in a summer garden, like the glow of the northern lights painting the night skies, like ocean waves crashing with untamed power against ancient rocks…

I LOVE soft kisses!!

— V.
