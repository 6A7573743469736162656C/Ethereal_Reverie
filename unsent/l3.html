March 1, 2025

It’s strange how, with the setting sun, sorrow quietly slips into the heart.

Some might say I’m not acting "manly" enough. And maybe, in these moments, that’s true. But I believe it’s more important to be real. I’m tired of hiding behind masks. This is who I am.

They say you’re not supposed to be like this, that you have to be strong… But what if real strength is found in truth? In honesty… first and foremost with yourself. In allowing yourself to feel everything fully, without numbing it, without running away from the so-called "problems"?

And so, once again, I watch the sun sink beneath the horizon, taking the warmth with it, leaving only silence behind. And in that silence, melancholy sits beside me, looks me in the eyes like an old enemy… or perhaps a friend… One who knows far too much about me.

— V.
